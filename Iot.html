Internet of things

Gartner defines The Internet of Things (IoT) "is the network of physical objects that contain embedded technology to communicate and sense or interact with their internal states or the external environment."
As per International Data Corporation(IDC) press release "The Internet of Things Is Poised to Change Everything", The Internet of Things(IoT) represents a new contruct in the information and communications technology(ICT) world that is occupying the minds of IT vendors, service providers, and system integrators as it represents huge potential for new streams of revenue and new customers.
